## Assembly Guide (Rat Version)

The rat-adapted version of the Training Village was originally developed by the [Animal Minds Lab](https://www.uab.cat/en/incuab/fx-animalmindslab-en) and features significant structural modifications. Due to the larger physical size and superior strength of rats compared to mice, the framework relies heavily on robust, laser-cut acrylic paneling rather than 3D-printed components, minimizing the overall count of printed parts. Additionally, the operational corridor dimensions have been substantially scaled up to accommodate larger subjects safely and comfortably.

For detailed engineering schematics, bill of materials, and manufacturing files for this specific version, please contact us directly.
